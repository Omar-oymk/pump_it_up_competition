4. PremiumPulse Challenge: Predicting Financial Risk
(Regression)

4.1 Link https://www.kaggle.com/competitions/premiumpulse-risk-
modeling/overview

4.2 Challenge Description
Develop a regression modelthat estimates the monetary cost of financial coverage
products based on a variety of customer attributes.
4.3 Data Description
The data used in this challenge (training and test sets) was produced using a generative
model inspired by real-world financial risk assessment data. While the feature distributions
resemble those from public datasets,they have been intentionally altered. You may
optionally compare with related open datasets to explore how differences affect
performance or enrich your model through data augmentation.
